Morning

Lake edge, light wind, low clouds

Thin ripples moved east across the water while the reeds stayed mostly still. The whole shoreline felt suspended.

Afternoon

Main street after rain

Pavement held the sky in fragments. Store signs were brighter in the reflection than they were overhead.

Evening

Last train through the cut

Sound arrived before the lights. The track kept humming for a few seconds after everything else had gone dark again.
